Prayer Changes Everything

Prayer

At PCE Church, we believe that prayer is not only a vital part of our relationship with God, but it is also a transformative force that shapes our lives and community. Prayer is a powerful tool that opens the door to God's presence, allowing us to communicate directly with our Creator. The power of prayer lies in its ability to invite divine intervention, shifting the atmosphere and circumstances around us. Jesus taught His disciples to pray persistently and with faith, assuring them that God listens, responds, and acts according to His perfect will. Prayer is not limited to asking for our needs, but it is also an opportunity to offer thanks, praise, and surrender our desires to God's greater plan. It aligns our hearts with His purposes and reminds us of His sovereignty and faithfulness. .

Changes

Changes are the catalysts of growth, pushing us beyond our comfort zones and inviting new perspectives. They can be daunting, yet they are essential for transformation, whether in our personal lives, relationships, or the world around us. Embracing change means accepting the ebb and flow of life, understanding that each shift offers valuable lessons. In navigating changes, we discover resilience, adaptability, and the potential for a brighter future.

Everything

Everything is the vast tapestry of existence, encompassing our experiences, dreams, and connections. It is the interplay of moments that shape our realities and define our purpose. Recognizing that everything is interconnected empowers us to appreciate the beauty in the mundane and the extraordinary. Our choices, thoughts, and actions ripple through this tapestry, reminding us that each small act can have a profound impact on the world. Embracing this truth inspires us to live intentionally, with love and compassion at the forefront of our journey.

Our Story

How did it all start...?

  • 3 September 2000

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our journey began in humble surroundings, starting in a small shack where a handful of devoted members gathered to share their faith and build community. As we grew, we moved to an old age home, embracing the warmth of fellowship among seniors who welcomed our presence. Our next chapter took us to a former tavern, where the vibrant spirit of our congregation transformed the space into a sanctuary of worship. Each step of the way, we found purpose and connection, and eventually, we were blessed to find our current home—a place where we can continue to grow, serve, and spread love in our community.

  • March 2017

    Finding land

    Finding a permanent piece of land for our church is crucial for fostering a sense of stability and belonging within our community. Owning our own space allows us to establish roots, create a dedicated sanctuary for worship, and develop programs that can grow and thrive over time. Relying on rentals or favors can lead to uncertainty and limitations, impacting our ability to serve our congregation fully. A permanent home not only reflects our commitment to our mission but also empowers us to build a lasting legacy for future generations.

  • December 2018

    Building a home

    Building our home was a true labor of love, as our congregation came together to transform our new land into a welcoming space for worship and community. After the church structure was completed, we rolled up our sleeves and got to work. We cleared the land of weeds, making way for gardens and pathways that would invite people in. Together, we constructed roads to ensure accessibility, and erected pallesites for gatherings and events. This hands-on effort not only strengthened our bonds as a community but also instilled a sense of pride and ownership in our shared space—a testament to our dedication and unity in building a vibrant home for all.

  • September 2023

    24 Years Later

    Over the past 24 years, our church has flourished in both faith and numbers, a testament to the unwavering dedication of our community. As we gathered in our humble beginnings, we nurtured a culture of love, support, and spiritual growth that resonated with those who walked through our doors. Through heartfelt worship, impactful outreach programs, and meaningful connections, we have welcomed new members into our fold, each contributing to our vibrant tapestry. Our growth reflects not only the strength of our beliefs but also our commitment to serving others and fostering a welcoming environment where everyone can experience the transformative power of faith. As we celebrate this milestone, we look forward to continuing our journey together, united in purpose and love.

Bishop & Wife

Mr.& Mrs F. Sekgothodi

Bishop who oversees everything in the church holds a vital role that encompasses spiritual leadership, administration, and pastoral care. This includes guiding and mentoring clergy and laity in their spiritual journeys, preaching the Word of God, and ensuring doctrinal integrity. The bishop is responsible for the administration of church programs and resources, ensuring compliance with established policies. They provide pastoral care to congregants, especially during times of crisis, and lead worship services, administering sacraments like baptism and communion. Additionally, the bishop fosters community engagement, promoting the church's mission beyond its walls and encouraging outreach initiatives, while also mentoring other clergy, overseeing their appointments and evaluations, and addressing any disciplinary matters as needed. Overall, the bishop plays a crucial role in nurturing the spiritual health of the congregation and the broader community.

Prophet & Wife

Mr. & Mrs H.T Mathibe.

The prophet and his wife overseeing two church branches in Khutshong Carletonville and Kagiso Krugersdorp serve as essential spiritual leaders, guiding their congregations with vision and compassion. Together, they are responsible for fostering a vibrant worship environment, where they preach, teach, and inspire their members to deepen their faith. They offer pastoral care, providing support to individuals and families during times of need, and encourage active involvement in community outreach and service projects. Their leadership extends to organizing and coordinating church events, ensuring that both branches function harmoniously while maintaining a unified mission. By nurturing relationships within and beyond their congregations, they cultivate a sense of belonging and purpose, empowering members to grow spiritually and contribute positively to their communities. Through their collaborative efforts, they exemplify a strong partnership in ministry, balancing the demands of leadership with the love and support that strengthens both church branches.
